# 📖 Customer Portal User Guide

**ChemInvoice Pro - Customer Invoice Portal**

---

## ✅ Quick Start (60 seconds)

### Step 1: Open Customer Portal
Go to: `https://cheminvoice.pk/customer-portal`

### Step 2: Enter Your Email
- Enter the email address where you receive invoices
- Click "Send Login Code"

### Step 3: Check Your Email
- Look for email from "ChemInvoice Pro"
- Check spam folder if not in inbox
- Copy the **6-digit code**

### Step 4: Enter Code
- Paste the 6-digit code in the portal
- Click "Verify & Login"

### Step 5: View Your Invoices
✅ Done! You now have access to all your invoices.

---

## 📋 Dashboard Overview

Once logged in, you'll see:

### 1. **Total Invoices** Card
- Shows how many invoices you have

### 2. **Total Amount** Card
- Sum of all your invoice amounts

### 3. **Paid** Card (Green)
- Total amount you've already paid

### 4. **Outstanding** Card (Red)
- Amount still pending payment

---

## 📄 Invoice List

The table below the stats shows all your invoices:

| Column | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Invoice # | Unique invoice number |
| Date | When invoice was issued |
| Description | Number of items in invoice |
| Amount | Total invoice amount in PKR |
| Status | PAID or PENDING |
| Action | Download PDF button |

---

## 💾 How to Download Invoice

1. Find the invoice in the list
2. Click **"📄 View"** button on the right
3. Download opens automatically
4. Save to your computer
5. Forward to your accountant or records

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: I don't see my email in the system?
**A:** Your company must send you at least one invoice first. After that, your email will be registered automatically.

### Q: My code expired, what do I do?
**A:** No problem! Go back and click "Use Different Email" to request a new code. Previous codes expire after 10 minutes.

### Q: I didn't receive the email?
**A:** 
1. Check your spam folder
2. Check promotions tab (if using Gmail)
3. Wait 2-3 minutes for email to arrive
4. Try requesting code again

### Q: How long does my session last?
**A:** Your session stays active for 24 hours. After that, you'll need to login again.

### Q: Can I see invoices from other companies?
**A:** No, you can only see invoices from your company.

### Q: Can I print the invoice?
**A:** Yes! Download the PDF and print it. It's in professional invoice format.

### Q: How do I pay the invoice?
**A:** Contact your company's payment department. The portal is just for viewing invoices.

### Q: Is my data secure?
**A:** Yes! 
- Emails are verified (only you can access your account)
- All data is encrypted
- Invoice access is restricted to you
- Servers use professional security standards

## 🔒 Security Tips

✅ **DO:**
- Use your real email address
- Keep your login codes private
- Logout when done on shared computers
- Report suspicious emails to your company

❌ **DON'T:**
- Share your login code with others
- Click links in suspicious emails
- Use weak passwords (if you set one)
- Save login info on public computers

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### Problem: "Failed to send code"
**Solution:** 
- Check your internet connection
- Make sure email is correct
- Wait a moment and try again

### Problem: "Invalid code"
**Solution:**
- Check you entered it correctly (numbers only)
- Make sure you're using the latest code
- Old codes expire after 10 minutes

### Problem: "Session expired"
**Solution:**
- You'll be logged out automatically after 24 hours
- Just login again with your email

### Problem: "Invoice not found"
**Solution:**
- Make sure invoice was issued to your email
- Check that you're logged in with correct email
- Contact company to confirm
